About Us

Bright Stars Global Initiative (BSGI) is a visionary NGO dedicated to creating a lasting global impact. Guided by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, we empower youth, foster innovation, promote peace, and advocate for good health and education across communities worldwide.
